Joining Us

We are actively recruiting high-quality postdoctoral fellows and graduate students. We will give priority to eligible applicants who have an educational background in Statistics, Biostatistics, and Bioinformatics, and who would like to dedicate their future research into the area of statsitical genetics. Successful candidates will join us and will work with me to develop and apply novel statistics methods and bioinformatics tools for gene-gene/gene-environment interaction research and risk prediction research. Applicants with programming skills in R and/or C/C++ are preferred, but not required. If you are interested in working with us, please contact me via e-mail with your CV and a brief description of your interest.

Postdoctoral Fellowship

We are currently looking for applications for a postdoctoral fellowship position in tobacco and other drug dependence-related biostatistics/bioinformatics research.
